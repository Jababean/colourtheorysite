Colour Schemes

Complementary Colours

Complementary colours are the simplest type of colour scheme, involving only two colours.

Complementary colours are any two colours that are directly opposite of each other on the colour wheel. As direct opposites, complementary colours contrast well together. This makes images bold and distinctive, allowing the image to draw attention to itself.

This colour scheme does not use many colours. It’s important to remember that sometimes, less is more. As complementary colour schemes are simpler, they are easier to look at.

Example: Blue and Orange
