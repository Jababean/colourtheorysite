Colour Harmony

Certain colours appear nicer together, which is the foundation of colour harmony. Certain colour schemes have are harmonious. The 5 main colour schemes are:

  • Complementary
  • Split Complementary
  • Analogous
  • Triadic
  • Tetradic

In addition to the three main colour schemes, there are split complementary and tetradic colour schemes.

Read more: https://zevendesign.com/

Complementary

Colours that are direct opposite of each other

Split Complementary

Two colours adjacent to the complement colour

Analogous

Colours next to each other on a colour wheel

Triadic

3 colours spaced evenly along the colour wheel

Tetradic

4 colours spaced evenly along the colour wheel
