Colour Theory

Hue

Colour hue is the colour part of a color. In technical terms, it’s the properties of a stimuli (or image) that is perceived to be a certain colour. For example, objects that appear red have a red hue.

Saturation

Saturation is the intensity of a colour hue. A pale red has low saturation, where a bright red will be highly saturated.

Shade

Shade is a type of saturation where black has been added to a colour hue. This makes the colour darker.

Tint

Tint is the opposite of shade. Tint is a type of saturation where white has been added to a colour hue.
